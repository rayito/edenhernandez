Music and Dance Festival PromoVideo promo for the Granada International Festival of Music and Dance

Client: Design school project
Target: Mostly middle-aged people, with high interest in culture and above-average income.
Categories: Motion Graphics / Video editing

Leitmotiv

In this festival, classical and flamenco (both music and dance) are tied and connected. I decided to tie and connect classical clips with flamenco clips using ribbons as the common element that flies and navigates through the scenes. It became the leitmotiv of this promo.
