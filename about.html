Hi.

I'm Edén Hernández.

I'm an Andalusian designer born in Granada, raised in Cádiz (southern Spain).

I've been a consultant, developer and UX designer until I became a freelance graphic designer.

I enjoy working with people, helping them find their visual identity and creating audiovisual experiences.

I love music, movies and basically all kinds of art. I always have a guitar close to me, but I also find happiness in front of a piano and behind a video camera.

I'm veggie and I love good, healthy food, craft beers and Spanish wine.
